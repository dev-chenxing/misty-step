# Misty Step

Misty Step is a Morrowind mod built with MWSE-Lua that provides a short-range teleportation spell effect. It creates a "Misty Step" spell and scroll.

**Key features**

- Adds a `Misty Step` spell that teleports the caster up to ~60 feet (stops before collisions).
- Two targeting modes: `camera` (aim at where you land on the floor) and `facing` (aim in the direction the caster is facing). Configurable via MWSE MCM.

**How to get it**

- The spell is added to some spell merchants, for example, Llathyno Hlaalu in Balmora Temple and Sirilonwe in Vivec Mages Guild.
- A `Scroll of Misty Step` is added to some scoll merchants，such as Folms Mirel in Caldera Mages Guild and Tanar Llervi in Ald'ruhn Mages Guild.
- The full list of merchants that sell the spell and scroll can be found and configured in the MCM.
- The scroll also has a small chance to appear in random scroll loot.
